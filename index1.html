pet care routines

We're basically explaining to pet owners th best routinds to follow to carefully and train your pets

Dogs

    Daily

  • Feeding:Provide appropriate portion of high-quality dog food
  • Walks:Take your dog for at least one walk (or playtime if you have a yard)
  • Playtime:Engage in play with toys or games for mental stimulation
  • Training:Spend a few minutes on basic commands or tricks

    weekly

  • Grooming:Brush your dog’s coat to reduce shedding and check for mats.
  • Nail trimming:Trim nails if needed (every 2-4 weeks, depending on wear)
  • Ear cleaning:Check and clean ears to prevent infections

    Monthly

  • Flea and tick prevention:Administer preventive medication as recommended.
  • Vet checkup:Schedule a check-up or vaccination if due.
  • Deep cleaning:Wash bedding and toys, and clean living areas.

Cats

    Daily

  • Feeding: Offer fresh food and clean water.
  • Litter Box: Scoop the litter box at least once daily.
  • Playtime: Spend time playing with your cat to encourage exercise.

    Weekly

  • Litter Box Maintenance: Change litter and clean the box thoroughly.
  • Grooming: Brush your cat’s coat, especially if they have long hair.

    Monthy

  • Flea Prevention: Administer any flea and tick treatments.
  • Vet Visit: Schedule a vet visit if vaccinations or check-ups are due.
  • Toys Inspection: Check toys for wear and replace if necessary.

Small animals

    Daily

  • Feeding: Provide fresh hay, vegetables, and pellets.
  • Clean Water: Ensure fresh water is always available.
  • Social Interaction: Spend time handling and playing with your pet.

    Weekly

  • Cage Cleaning: Remove waste and clean the cage.
  • Grooming: Brush your pet to remove loose fur and check for health issues.

    Monthly

  • Vet Check-Up: Schedule a health check or vaccination if needed.
  • Toys Refresh: Rotate or replace toys to keep your pet engaged.
